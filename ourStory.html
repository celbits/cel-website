The beginning

It all started in 2003

The idea for CEL was seeded by the BITSAA Center for Entrepreneurship and Venture Capital (CEVC) with the vision of promoting the spirit of entrepreneurial thinking among the BITSian community . It was started in the year 2003 by Anupendra Sharma, who went on to become one of the founding members of Startup Leadership Program later in 2006.

The concerted efforts of the students, alumni and faculty resulted in the Center (within a short span of 6 months) being named as one of the top centres of entrepreneurship among Indian Universities
